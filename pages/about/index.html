The PahadiKart Story

From Mountain Dreams to Digital Reality

Born in the heart of Uttarakhand, PahadiKart began as a simple dream: to connect the skilled artisans of the Himalayas with the world. Our founder, having grown up watching the incredible craftsmanship of local communities, noticed how these masterpieces remained hidden in remote villages.

In 2023, we started with just 10 artisans from 3 villages. Today, we've grown into a platform that empowers over 500+ artisans across 25+ Himalayan villages, preserving traditional crafts that have been passed down through generations.
